Why you should work with me

  • Design with Purpose

    By working together we can design something which makes sense for your specific audience.

    We can also work from your existing designs and, if necessary, iterate on them to create something fully responsive and accessible.

  • Accessible

    From design to code, I consider accessibility very important. By following Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG), your website will be easy to understand and navigate for all types of users.

  • Responsive

    Taking a mobile-first approach, your website will work seamlessly between different devices and viewports.

  • Performance

    Being bespoke, your website won't be weighed down by features that you don't need.

    Tools such as Google's Lighthouse will be used to measure how quickly your website takes to load.
